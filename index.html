For Families in Grief

Have you lost a loved one?

This might be new to you, but we can help. When everyone is in grief, it can feel overwhelming to have rushed conversations about who gets what. The only urgent issue after a death is making sure their final resting wishes are honored. Everything else can wait until you understand the process.

Understanding the process after a death makes sure everyone is treated fairly and no legal steps are missed. In simple cases, you may only need to contact the bank. In complex ones, taxes, trust laws, real estate, and probate filings all come into play.

A note for executors

If there are disagreements about how to do things or who is in charge, rushing decisions might make you personally liable for anything not done exactly according to the law.

Take this time to learn your rights. We've walked hundreds of Arizona executors and trustees through this. The first 30 minutes is free, and you'll leave knowing what's actually urgent and what isn't.
