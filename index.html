What We Do

Comprehensive Estate Planning Services

From your first will to complex trust administration, Keystone guides Arizona families through every step of protecting what matters most.

Estate Planning

A comprehensive estate plan is the cornerstone of protecting your family. We craft personalized wills, trusts, powers of attorney, and healthcare directives tailored to your specific goals and Arizona law. Whether your estate is modest or complex, we ensure every asset is accounted for and every wish is honored.

Living Trusts

Avoid probate entirely and transfer assets to your loved ones without court delays. A revocable living trust gives you control during your lifetime and certainty for your family after.

Probate Administration

Navigating Arizona probate courts is complex and emotionally taxing. We handle every filing, creditor notice, and distribution so your family can focus on healing.

Learn More

Trust Administration

Acting as a trustee carries real legal duties. We guide successor trustees through their obligations, ensuring assets are distributed correctly and beneficiaries are protected.

Learn More

Long-Term Care Planning

Protecting your assets from the high cost of care is one of the most overlooked parts of estate planning. We help families preserve Medicaid eligibility and retirement savings.

Learn More

Special Needs Planning

A Special Needs Trust ensures a loved one with disabilities receives financial support without disqualifying them from essential government benefits. We build these with care and precision.

Probate Litigation

When disputes arise over a will or trust, experienced advocacy matters. We represent clients in contested probate proceedings, protecting your rightful inheritance and your family relationships where possible.
